Welcome to BYIF

Bangladesh Youth ICT Forum (BYIF) is a youth-driven platform dedicated to fostering innovation, digital skills, and ICT education across Bangladesh. We connect and empower the next generation of tech leaders to build a smarter, digital future.

About Us

We aim to educate, inspire, and connect the youth of Bangladesh with the power of Information & Communication Technology. Through workshops, seminars, and real-world projects, we nurture future tech leaders.

Our Projects

Youth Coding Camp

A hands-on coding camp for students across Bangladesh to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.

ICT Olympiad

Annual competition to inspire young minds in ICT through quizzes, apps, and innovation challenges.
